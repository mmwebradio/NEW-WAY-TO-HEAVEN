Death Penalty Action works
to stop executions and abolish
the death penalty through advocacy,
education, and action.


The Abolitionist Action Committee
is an ad-hoc group of individuals committed to
highly visible and effective public education
for alternatives to the death penalty through
nonviolent direct action.
